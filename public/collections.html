    Caring for Your Diamond Jewelry

    Regular Cleaning

    Clean your diamond jewelry regularly with warm water and mild soap. Use a soft brush to gently remove dirt and oils that can diminish sparkle.

    Proper Storage

    Store each piece separately in a soft-lined jewelry box to prevent scratches. Keep diamonds away from other jewelry that might damage them.

    Professional Maintenance

    Schedule annual professional cleanings and inspections. We'll check settings, clean thoroughly, and ensure your jewelry remains in perfect condition.

    Activity Precautions

    Remove jewelry during activities that might expose it to chemicals, extreme temperatures, or physical impact. This protects both the diamonds and settings.
