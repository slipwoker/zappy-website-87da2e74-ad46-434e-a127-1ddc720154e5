    Our Commitment to Ethical Sourcing

    The jewelry industry has historically been associated with concerning practices, from conflict diamonds funding violence to environmental damage from irresponsible mining. At Dror Lahav Jewelry Design, we're committed to being part of the solution. Our dedication to ethically sourced engagement rings and responsible practices reflects our belief that beautiful jewelry should never come at an unacceptable human or environmental cost.

    Every diamond we offer is certified conflict-free and sourced from suppliers who adhere to the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme. But we go further than basic compliance. We actively seek out suppliers who demonstrate commitment to fair labor practices, environmental stewardship, and community development in mining regions. We visit supplier facilities, review third-party audits, and maintain ongoing relationships that allow us to verify ethical practices throughout the supply chain.

    We're transparent about our sourcing practices and happy to provide detailed information about where your diamond comes from. For clients who want even more assurance, we can source diamonds with additional certifications or from specific origins. Some of our diamonds come from Canadian mines known for their high environmental and labor standards, while others are vintage or antique stones that carry no new mining impact.

    This commitment to ethical sourcing doesn't mean compromising on quality or paying premium prices. We've built relationships with ethical suppliers that allow us to offer competitive pricing on responsibly sourced diamonds. We believe everyone should be able to purchase jewelry that reflects their values, regardless of budget.
